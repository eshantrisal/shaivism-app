ॐ नमः शिवाय

Shiva —

is he a god you pray to?

Or the very awareness

reading these words right now?

A thousand years ago, deep in the mountains of Kashmir,

a radical truth was whispered among sages:

You are not searching for Shiva.

You are Shiva, pretending to search.

Every thought, every breath, every heartbeat —

consciousness, dancing as the universe.

This is Kashmir Shaivism.

Not a religion. Not a philosophy.

A remembering.
